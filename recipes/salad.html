Chicken Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 head of romaine lettuce, washed and torn into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmessan cheese
  • Caesar dressing

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Grill or pan-fry them until cooked through. Let them cool and then slice into thin strips.
  2. In a large salad bowl, combine the romaine lettuce, croutons, and grated Parmesan cheese.
  3. Add the sliced chicken on top of the salad.
  4. Drizzcle Caesar dressing over the salad and toss well to coat.
  5. Serve immediately.
