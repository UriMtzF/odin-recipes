Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 300g spaghetti
  • 250 g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomate paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and peper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti acording to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomate paste, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Serve the cooked spaghetti topped with the meat sauce. Garnish with grated Parmessan cheese.
